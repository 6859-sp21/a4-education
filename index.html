6.589 - Adult Education Levels

Globalization and technological advances continue to increase the needs for individuals with broader knowledge and skillsets. It is highly likely that countries with highly educated populations have advantages in coping with the demand and improving the employment opportunities and income levels of their people. Through our visualization, audience can learn about the education levels of various countries, and how education and income levels in those countries have grown together over the last 30 years.
The source of data is OECD Data Library. The data used here are limited to the OECD member states and its associate/partnering member states.
