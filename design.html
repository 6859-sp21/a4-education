Design Decisions

Encoding: Bar Chart

1. At the beginning , we had two encoding options, "line chart" and "bar chart", because we want to convey "trend" and "comparison" from our visualization

2. After the discussion, we decided to focus on the comparison, so we chose "bar chart" as basic encoding.

Message we want to tell - Overview and Drill Down

1. For the overview page, we use “bar chart race.” The animation race allows the audience intuitively understand the overall situation for the education indicator year by year

2. Also, we provide a detailed drill-down page for the year 2019 for readers who want to understand the latest situation in the countries.

3. We also believe that an interactive drill-down page can build “trust” with the reader because they can explore the data by themselves.
